New Regulations

City Council Approves New Regulations for Taxi Transportation Services

The city council has approved a new set of regulations for taxi transportation services in our community. The new rules include mandatory background checks for drivers, a cap on the number of ride-sharing vehicles allowed on the road, and an increase in licensing fees. The aim of the legislation is to improve safety for passengers and create a more level playing field for traditional taxi companies.
